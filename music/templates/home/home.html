Spootify is a Web Application that assists users to discover and rediscover Music.


With Spootify you will be able to browse tracks, retrieve information about your chosen tracks, search tracks by Artists and Albums, and many more features.


To the left of the screen is a navigation bar to assist with roaming through Spootify.

You will see ways to return to the Home page, Register, Logging in, and Logging out. You will also see ways to Browse Tracks, Search by Artist, and Search by Artists which are all conveniently sorted in Alphabetical order.


To the right of the screen is a list of Popular Tracks to help you discover what tracks other people are listening to and interacting with.
